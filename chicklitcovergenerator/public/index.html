About ChickLit Cover Generator

Chick lit is the expanded universe of Sex and the City. It’s the foundational text of modern feminine aspirational consumerism. It’s not being able to afford Jimmy Choo shoes. It’s wearing a real Burberry scarf with a knock-off Versace handbag and having a reason for doing that at hand. It’s every yogurt commercial you’ve ever seen. It’s pink and sparkly girl-versions of everything. It’s the fantasy of eventually getting your shit together and your eyeliner straight on the same day.

And you know what? It’s fun. It’s genre fiction. It’s light and playful and cliche. It’s fiction that falls somewhere between romance novels and fashion magazines, and it’s a whole bunch of stories written by women, for women, and that makes the marketing and presentation exactly as problematic as it sounds.

Which is what makes it all so easy to parody.
